Research Experience

  • Formation of two-dimensional quantum droplets, 2022.10-2023.09
    Research Advisor: Professor Hiroki SAITO
    Summary: Explored the stability of 2D quantum droplets in ultracold gases, focusing on the critical balance between dipole and contact interactions. Solved the extended Gross-Pitaevskii equation (eGPE) using the pseudospectral method to accurately determine the ground state of these droplets.
  • Cold Atom Interferometric Measurement of Gravitational Acceleration, 2022.07-2022.09
    Research Advisor: Professor Qiang Lin
    Summary: Investigated high-precision gravity measurements using cold atom interferometry. Developed an experimental setup to improve the accuracy of gravity acceleration measurements beyond conventional methods.
  • Motor Imagery Control of An Electroencephalic Robotic Arm, 2021.07-2022.06
    Research Advisor: Associate Professor Dezhao LI
    Summary: Investigated controlling the simple movement of a robotic arm by human imagination and extracting the features of collected EEG signals using neural networks.
